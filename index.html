RICS (Royal Institute of Chartered Surveyors)LONDON AWARDS 2015

We are very pleased to announce that at the RICS Award ceremony last night in London we were awarded 2 Highly Commended Awards. These Awards recognise property professionals and showcase their talent and ingenuity across various categories - the winners of Building Conservation, Community Benefit, Design through Innovation, Infrastructure and Regeneration.

The projects awarded Highly Commended are:

  • The pavilion portico at The Oval Cricket Ground, London, designed by Hugh Petter
  • The new housing at Bentley Priory, Middlesex, designed by Nigel Anderson
